About the FDP

The Faculty Development Programme (FDP) on Process-centric and Reward-based Interpretable Models in Explainable AI (PRIMEAI-2025) is a comprehensive 8-day programme meticulously designed to provide participants with in-depth knowledge of Machine Learning (ML), Deep Learning (DL), and Explainable AI (XAI).

This FDP addresses contemporary challenges in AI by emphasizing process-based reward interpretability, combining state-of-the-art ML/DL techniques with explainable AI frameworks. The programme focuses on making AI models transparent, accountable, and trustworthy - essential attributes for modern AI applications in critical domains like healthcare, finance, and autonomous systems.

Participants will gain hands-on experience with cutting-edge tools and frameworks including Captum, What-If Tool, TensorBoard, SHAP, LIME, and more. Learn from distinguished faculty from premier international institutions (University of Ljubljana), top IIITs (Allahabad, Lucknow), and industry leaders from Google Cloud Platform and Microsoft Japan.

Key Training Areas

  • ML/DL Foundations: Core algorithms, neural networks, feature engineering, and evaluation metrics
  • Explainable AI (XAI): SHAP, LIME, Integrated Gradients, DeepLIFT, Grad-CAM, and attention mechanisms
  • PRIMEAI Framework: Process + Reward + Interpretability approach for transparent AI systems
  • Advanced DL: CNNs, RNNs/LSTMs, Transformers, GANs, and LLMs with interpretability
  • Reinforcement Learning: Reward modeling, policy gradients, and multi-agent RL
  • Industry Applications: Healthcare diagnostics, financial fraud detection, autonomous systems, and NLP
  • XAI Tools: Hands-on with Captum, What-If Tool, TensorBoard, and visualization frameworks
  • Future Directions: Causal inference, counterfactuals, foundation models, and ethical AI

About MMMUT Gorakhpur
