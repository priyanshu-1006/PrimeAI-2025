About FDP PRIMEAI-2025

The Process Reward Interpretability Model with Explainable AI (PRIMEAI-2025) is a eleven-session faculty development program designed to provide participants with in-depth knowledge of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Explainable AI, with a focus on making AI models transparent, accountable, and trustworthy.

As AI systems become increasingly complex, traditional black-box models, such as deep neural networks, often lack interpretability, making it difficult for researchers and practitioners to understand decision-making processes, verify outcomes, or ensure alignment with intended objectives. This FDP addresses these challenges by emphasising process-based reward interpretability, combining state-of-the-art ML/DL techniques with explainable AI frameworks.
