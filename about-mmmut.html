Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT), Gorakhpur, formerly known as Madan Mohan Malaviya Engineering College (MMMEC), is a premier technical institution in Eastern Uttar Pradesh. Established in 1962, the institute has been at the forefront of engineering education and research for over six decades.

The university was renamed and elevated to university status in 2013 as a tribute to the great nationalist and educationist Pandit Madan Mohan Malaviya. Located in the historic city of Gorakhpur, MMMUT serves as a beacon of technical education in the region.
